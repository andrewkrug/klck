# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 2026-05-20_

Klck is a metronome. It does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information about you. This page exists because the App Store requires every app to publish a privacy policy — Klck's is short because there is essentially nothing to disclose.

## What information Klck collects

**None.** Klck has no user accounts, no analytics, no crash reporting, no advertising SDKs, no third-party network services, and does not contact any remote server during normal operation.

## How Klck uses the microphone

Klck includes a chromatic instrument tuner. When you tap **LISTEN** on the tuner panel, Klck requests permission to use the device microphone solely to estimate the fundamental pitch of the sound it picks up. The audio:

- Is processed entirely on-device using autocorrelation.
- Is never recorded to a file.
- Is never transmitted to a server or any third party.
- Is discarded as soon as it has been analyzed for pitch.

You can revoke microphone access at any time in **Settings ▸ Privacy & Security ▸ Microphone ▸ Klck**.

## How Klck stores your settings

Presets, setlists, and your in-app preferences are saved as small JSON files inside Klck's own app sandbox on your device (in iOS's `Application Support` directory, or the equivalent on macOS). These files never leave your device. Deleting Klck removes them automatically.

## Network usage

Klck makes no network requests. It will continue to function with no network connection.

## Third-party services

Klck does not embed any third-party SDKs, analytics frameworks, ad networks, or telemetry libraries.

## Children's privacy

Klck does not collect data from anyone of any age. Apple has rated it for ages **4+**.

## Changes to this policy

If Klck's privacy practices ever change, this page will be updated and the **Last updated** date above will reflect the change. Historical versions are available through the [GitHub history of this file](https://github.com/andrewkrug/klck/commits/main/docs/privacy.md).
